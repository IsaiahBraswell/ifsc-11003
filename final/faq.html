FAQ

How many Super Bowls have the Steelers won?

The Steelers have won six Super Bowls, tied for the most in NFL history.

Where do the Steelers play?

The team plays at Acrisure Stadium, located on Pittsburgh’s North Shore.

Who is the Steelers’ biggest rival?

The Baltimore Ravens are widely considered the Steelers’ biggest rival.

What are the team colors?

Black and gold — representing Pittsburgh’s industrial heritage.

Why is the logo only on one side of the helmet?

The Steelers originally tested the logo on one side and kept it because fans loved the unique look.
